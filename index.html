Engineering Profile

I am an Electrical Engineer with a strong focus on designing and building intelligent systems where hardware, software, and data intersect. My work spans embedded electronics, custom PCB design, IoT architectures, and AI-driven perception systems deployed on real-world platforms.

Through industry experience, research-driven hackathons, and hands-on prototyping, I have worked on systems that range from sensor-rich environmental monitoring and smart infrastructure to autonomous drones and image-based analytical pipelines. I enjoy working close to the hardware layer, understanding system constraints such as latency, power consumption, reliability, and deployment conditions, and translating them into robust engineering solutions.

I am particularly interested in roles that involve applied R&D, embedded intelligence, robotics, and sustainable technology—where engineering decisions directly impact system performance and real-world outcomes.

Core Technical Strengths

My technical background is built around full-system thinking rather than isolated components. I am comfortable moving from schematic design and PCB layout to firmware development, data processing, and system-level integration.
